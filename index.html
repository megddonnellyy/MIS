Welcome to EcoCurls

Meet Our Founder

Meg Donnelly founded EcoCurls in 2025 with a mission to embrace natural beauty and sustainability. Her own journey with curls inspired a brand that celebrates all textures while caring for the planet within the beauty industry.

Why Use Us?

We’re not your average hair brand. EcoCurls is run by curl experts who actually understand textured hair and are passionate about bringing ethically sourced hair to the market using sustainable methods. We offer:

  • Plant-powered, cruelty-free products
  • Refill stations at select salons to reduce plastic waste
  • Affordable products with great quality
  • A test-strand policy that allows you to exchange youre hair for a perfect match for free!
  • A founder-led company that’s passionate about making sustainability accessible
